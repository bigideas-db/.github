## What is BigIdeasDB?

> **BigIdeasDB is an AI-powered platform that surfaces validated startup ideas from over 1 million real user complaints across 10 platforms** (Reddit, G2, Capterra, Upwork, the Apple App Store, and Google Play), then layers in verified revenue data, acquisition listings, and VC funding intelligence so every opportunity comes with proof of demand.

BigIdeasDB is an AI-powered research platform built from over 1 million real user complaints across 10 platforms. It analyzes Reddit discussions, G2 reviews, Capterra reviews, Upwork job postings, and app store feedback from the Apple App Store and Google Play, then combines that with verified revenue data, acquisition listings, and funding intelligence to hand you validated startup opportunities.

Most startups fail because they build something nobody wants. BigIdeasDB removes the guesswork by surfacing real problems people are already complaining about online, then helping you validate, plan, and build products with proof of demand behind them.

The platform covers the entire journey with 40+ databases, pipelines, and tools: discover validated opportunities, research the market, check the revenue potential, plan your roadmap, and ship.

# Frequently Asked Questions

**What is BigIdeasDB?**
BigIdeasDB is an AI-powered product-research and idea-validation platform that analyzes 1M+ real user complaints across Reddit, G2, Capterra, Upwork, and the app stores to help founders discover validated startup ideas, then plan and build them. It is free to start.

**How is BigIdeasDB different from doing my own research?**
It aggregates and AI-scores 1M+ data points across 10 platforms instantly. What would take weeks of manual scrolling is available in seconds, with every problem carrying severity, frequency, and market-gap scores, plus a direct link to the original source.

**What is a validated startup idea?**
An idea tied to a documented, recurring, high-severity complaint that people already pay (badly) to solve. It is the opposite of a brainstormed guess. BigIdeasDB surfaces these bottom-up, from real evidence.

**Is BigIdeasDB a GummySearch or Reddit API alternative?**
Yes. The BigIdeasDB MCP server is a drop-in Reddit API and PRAW alternative with no OAuth and no API keys, and it covers far more than Reddit (G2, Capterra, App Stores, Upwork, revenue, funding). GummySearch was Reddit-only and is shutting down in November 2026.

**How much does it cost?**
There is a free tier (tools, calculators, idea generator, limited browsing). Paid plans start at $99 lifetime, with full Pro access at $349 lifetime or $45/month, including databases, hosted MCP, Revenue Intelligence, BuildHub, and priority support.

**Is the data current?**
Yes. Databases update continuously, TrustMRR revenue is scraped daily, and Reddit pipelines run up to 5 times per day.
